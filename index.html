About us

Our band was formed in 2023. We are a real family from Ukraine: Oleh - dad (guitar and vocals), Natalia - mom (keyboard, bass, and vocals), and our two charming daughters: Daria (saxophone, flute, and arrangements) and Maria (trumpet). We love music and love bringing joy, which is why our repertoire includes many cheerful covers of popular songs in jazz and gypsy-style interpretations. We live in Split (Croatia) at the moment. You can invite us as musical accompaniment for your event—whether it's a wedding, a birthday, or just for a good mood. We will be happy to come to you!
