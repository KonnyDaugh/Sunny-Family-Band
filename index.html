About us

Music that brings joy, warmth, and life to your event
We are a Ukrainian family music band from Split — a dad, a mom, and two talented daughters. For two years, we’ve been creating joyful and heartfelt atmospheres at events of all kinds — from intimate family gatherings to large celebrations.

Who we are:

  • Dad and mom – vocals, guitar, bass and rhythm
  • Daughters – professionally play the saxophone, flute, and trumpet
  • Together – we bring authentic live music filled with warmth and positive energy

We’ve performed at numerous events and concerts, including some of the finest restaurants in Cyprus,where guests appreciated our vibrant sound and heartfelt connection with the audience.

Perfect for:

  • Private parties: weddings, birthdays, anniversaries
  • Corporate events and city festivals
  • Art evenings at restaurants, cafes, and hotels
  • Family festivals and intimate concerts

Our repertoire:

  • Fun, popular hits
  • Light jazz and soulful melodies
  • Beloved classics with a fresh, heartfelt twist

Why people love us:

  • Genuine family energy and positive vibes
  • Live performance with a unique mix of guitar, keyboard, vocals, saxophone, trumpet, and flute
  • Experience performing at international venues
  • Professional, friendly, and easy to work with
  • Personalized approach to every event

Let’s make your celebration truly sunny!
We love playing for people and are always open to new opportunities and collaborations.
Get in touch — and let’s make your event unforgettable!
